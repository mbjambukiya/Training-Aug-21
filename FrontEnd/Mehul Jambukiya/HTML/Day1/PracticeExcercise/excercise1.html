Gujarat CM Replacement

On September 11,2021 Saturday,

Hr.Vijaybhai Rupani , Chief Minister of Gujarat State resigned from the CM Post.
and Hr.Bhupendrabhai Patel took charge for CM Post on September 12,2021.
Bhupendrabhai Patel was MLA of Ghatlodia,Ahmedabad and also worked under ExCM Hr.Anandiben Patel.
